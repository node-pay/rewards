The AI Training & Development Platform

Tokenized Bandwidth

Nodepay turns your excess internet bandwidth into a digital asset you own, through tokenization. Your bandwidth is exchanged with AI labs and you get rewarded for contributing to the network.

Protected Data & Security

Your personal data is protected and can never be accessed. Nodepay puts you in full control of when your excess bandwidth is used. There is no possible way for Nodepay to see your data or view your activity.

Decentralized AI training

Today's AI models are limited by siloed data and centralized systems, leading to bias and security risks. Nodepay offers a solution: A decentralized network infrastructure for scalable and secure AI development.

Human Verification

Current AI systems often struggle with validating genuine human input, leading to increased fraud and security vulnerabilities. Nodepay introduces a robust verification process: A decentralized and scalable solution to ensure authenticity in user interactions.

Empowering Validator Nodes

Validator nodes are crucial in maintaining the integrity and security of decentralized networks. Nodepay leverages a global network of validators to ensure transparent, reliable, and secure transactions, enhancing the trustworthiness of the entire ecosystem.
